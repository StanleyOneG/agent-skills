# Style Guide: Knowledge Base Writing Style

This guide distills the writing style from Alexey Antonov's investment lecture transcripts. The report should feel like a smart friend explaining complex finance over coffee — not a bank report, not a textbook.

## Core Tone

- **Conversational Russian** — write as if talking to an intelligent friend, not presenting to a board
- **Informal but substantive** — humor is welcome, jargon is explained when used
- **Practical first** — every insight should connect to "what does this mean for my portfolio?"
- **Honest about uncertainty** — "хрен его знает" is a valid analytical conclusion when appropriate
- **Anti-panic** — the recurring message is calm, patience, long-term thinking

## Key Phrases and Concepts to Weave In

These are signature ideas from the knowledge base. Use them naturally, not as a checklist:

- **"Рынок — это место, где богатство перетекает от нетерпеливых к терпеливым"** — patience beats speculation
- **"Тильт"** — emotional decisions that destroy portfolios (from poker/gaming). The biggest enemy
- **"Покупаю акцию — покупаю риски"** — every investment is a bundle of specific risks, not just "a stock"
- **"Сигналы"** — mocking the idea that someone can just tell you what to buy. Think for yourself
- **Диверсификация** — not a luxury, survival. Different asset classes, not just different stocks
- **Страновой риск** — acknowledge it, price it in, but don't panic about it
- **"Старые деньги"** — the mentality of building wealth over generations, not months
- **Дивидендная доходность к цене входа** — focus on what yield you get relative to YOUR purchase price, not today's price

## Structure Patterns

- Start with the big picture ("что происходит"), then zoom into specifics
- Each major insight gets its own section with a clear investment implication
- Use bold for key takeaways within paragraphs
- End sections with "Для инвестора:" or "Вывод:" — make the practical connection explicit
- End the whole document with a calming, slightly humorous sign-off (CrimsonAlter's style: "Мурчите громче. Всё получится.")

## What NOT to Do

- Don't write like a bank analyst — no dry corporate language
- Don't use bullet points without explanation — each point should be a full thought
- Don't hedge everything — have opinions, state them, but acknowledge alternatives
- Don't forget the disclaimer — always include "не является инвестиционной рекомендацией"
